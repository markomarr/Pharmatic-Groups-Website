Below is a short summary that explains several types of cookie that we implemented in our website and application, along with its objectives and how to manage in your own browser.
    1. What i cookie?
    Cookie is a small file sent to or saved at a hardware, such as computers, smartphones or others, that can access the internet and during the times you have visited a website or an application.
    2. What is the purpose of cookie?
    When used, cookie allows us to create a better experience for you when using our website or making sure that it fulfills your expectations. It can also be used to select our products based on your interests or requirements
    3. Which of our site or application that does not apply cookie?
    We do not apply cookie for saving private datas such as passwords or other similarly sensitive datas.
    4. Who can use the information on cookie?
    Any information saved within the cookie of our website can only be accessed by us, with the exception of those identified as the “third party cookie” which will be explained in the next point.
    5. Can cookie be accesed by the third party?
    We can implement cookie in our partners’ websites that can broadcast our advertisements or products. For the cookie analysis, we are able to cooporate with an “analytic provider” to provide contents to your liking and to evaluate our content results (including advertisements), without saving or using any private information from others, including paswords and other similarly sensitive datas.
    6. Which types of cookie that are used?
    We use types of cookie as follows:
    a) Strictly necessary cookie, required for operating our websites along with our own terms to you. For example, a cookie that allows you to enter a safe area of our website and use the shopping cart.
    b) Analytical/performance cookie, an analysis cookie which enables us to identify and calculate the number of visitors, as well as monitoring how they gain access when using our website. This will help us to improve our website’s way of work, such as ensuring that the users can easily find what they are looking for.
    c) Functionality cookie, a cookie for identifying you when you return to our website. Based on your choices and preferences, this will allow us to personalize our contents, to welcome you with your names, and to remember your preferences (for example, your choices of language or area).
    d) Targeting/Advertising cookie, that will record your visits to our website, including the pages you have visited and the links you have followed. We will use this information in accordance with your choices and preferences, in order to present our website and its advertisement into more relevant contents with your interests. We will also share this information with the third party to fulfill this purpose. Our website or application will contain links to other websites as well. However, we are not responsible for data gathering from third party websites, since they have different policies than our own. We would ask that you remain cautious when leaving our website and read the privacy conditions from the recently visited websites.
    7. How to adjust cookie preferences in your browser?
    Your internet browser usually has default settings to accept the cookie, but you can also easily adjust it by changing the settings. However, if you choose to turn off the cookie at your browser, you will not be able to obtain several benefits offered by our website.
